Welcome To Ride Sharing System

•The Ride Sharing Platform is a web-based application designed to facilitate the sharing of rides among riders

  1. •It serves as a centralized platform where users can connect with drivers and can book rides of their favourite vehicle.
  2. .The platform connects passengers with available drivers and vehicles, allowing them to request transportation services based on their desired pickup and drop-off locations.

   .The Car Booking System/Ride Sharing Platform aims to enhance transportation convenience, reduce individual vehicle usage, and promote cost-effective and environmentally friendly commuting options. It provides a user-friendly interface, efficient matching algorithms, and reliable tracking mechanisms to create a seamless and enjoyable experience for both passengers and drivers.
